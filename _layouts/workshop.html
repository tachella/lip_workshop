This workshop brings together researchers working on learning-based methods for inverse problems and physics, covering topics including:

  • Self-supervised and unsupervised learning for imaging
  • Uncertainty quantification and Bayesian approaches
  • Unrolled algorithms for inverse problems
  • Plug-and-play and diffusion models for image reconstruction
  • Theoretical foundations of learning-based reconstruction

The workshop will take place at ENS Lyon, France, on 14–15 September 2026.
